Good to Know

A few common questions.

01

Do I need any medical background?

No. Most volunteer roles are focused on outreach, events, and logistics — not clinical work.

02

Do I need to be a student?

Not at all. While AURA is student-run, community members of any age are welcome to get involved.

03

How much time does it take?

As little or as much as you'd like — from a single event to an ongoing role throughout the year.
