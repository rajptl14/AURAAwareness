Why It Matters

This started as a personal story, not a strategy.

AURA's founders didn't set out to build an organization — they set out to do something with what they'd witnessed firsthand: family members whose early diagnosis changed everything, and research showing how much timing matters.

"We wanted every dollar to go where it actually helps — funding the research and access that gets people screened sooner."
