Behind the Signal

Driven by purpose,
united for impact.

We are a group of students who believe that actionable awareness shouldn't wait until it's too late — because for most cancers, timing is everything.

AURA Awareness was formed out of a collective realization: while complex medical advancements continue to progress, basic accessible community fundraising can immediately shift survival numbers.

By channeling resources directly into established organizations like the American Cancer Society, we keep our operations transparent, lean, and intensely focused on the primary objective — empowering lives through early detection.

The Core Initiative

The people keeping the
pulse alive

Students from IMSA, UW-Madison, Case Western, and Georgia Tech — united by one mission.

Raj Patel
Co-Founder · UW-Madison

An incoming freshman at UW-Madison and IMSA alumnus, Raj aims to become a physician at the intersection of neuroscience and computer science. Research at Northwestern and UIC gave him hands-on experience in the lab and a deep understanding of how disease impacts real people — driving him to co-found AURA.

Aydin Syed
Co-Founder · Case Western Reserve

Incoming freshman at Case Western and IMSA alumnus. Aydin's mother and aunt both survived breast cancer thanks to early detection — that personal experience is what sparked AURA. He previously raised $1,500 for pediatric cancer research as a kid, and he's not done yet.

Krish Sav
Vice President · IMSA

Rising senior at IMSA pursuing neurosurgery and a PhD. Krish co-developed a project achieving 98.2% accuracy in diagnosing brain tumors from MRI scans. His research in oncology and neurology fuels his commitment to equitable access to early detection for underserved communities.

Max Ballesteros
Art Head · IMSA

IMSA student and the creative force behind AURA's visual identity. Max brings the mission to life through design, making sure every piece of AURA's presence communicates urgency, clarity, and hope.
