Fighting cancer through awareness, education, and early detection.

AURA Awareness was founded by students who have witnessed the impact that cancer has on individuals, families, and communities. Through education, advocacy, and fundraising, we aim to promote early detection and support efforts that improve outcomes for patients worldwide.
