Our Mission

The gap between noticed and
found too late.

AURA exists because most cancer outcomes are still decided by how early a signal is caught but too many signals go unheard.

We raise funds to aid early detection of cancers through our community. Every dollar we collect goes directly to the American Cancer Society to fund research, screenings, and education.

Our goal is to surpass $1,500 in donations. With your support, we can move that needle and help more people get the scans, checkups, and resources they need before it's too late.
