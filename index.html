Our Mission

The gap between noticed and found too late.

Most cancer outcomes are still decided by how early a signal is caught — and too many signals go unheard. AURA raises funds to aid early cancer detection in our community.

Every dollar we collect goes directly to the American Cancer Society, funding research, screenings, and public education. Our goal is to surpass $1,500 in donations so more people can get the scans, checkups, and resources they need before it's too late.
